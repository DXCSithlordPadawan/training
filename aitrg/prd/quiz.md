# AI Training for PRD - Knowledge Quiz

## Instructions

This comprehensive quiz tests your knowledge of AI-assisted Product Requirements Document creation. The quiz covers all 5 weeks of training material.

**Quiz Format**:
- 100 questions total
- Multiple choice (A, B, C, D)
- One correct answer per question
- Answers provided at the end

**Scoring**:
- 90-100: Proficient (90-100%)
- 80-89: Competent (80-89%)
- 70-79: Developing (70-79%)
- 60-69: Beginner (60-69%)
- Below 60: Needs review

**Time Limit**: 90 minutes (recommended)
