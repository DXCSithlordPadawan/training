# AI Training Documentation Programme
## Executive Summary

---

## Programme Overview

The **Artificial Intelligence Training Documentation (AITRG) Programme** is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade training package designed to equip professionals with the knowledge and practical skills necessary to effectively leverage artificial intelligence tools in modern business environments.

This self-study programme provides 187.5 hours (5 weeks at 37.5 hours/week) of structured learning covering:
- Advanced prompt engineering techniques
- Claude Code mastery for agentic software development
- Microsoft 365 Copilot integration across productivity applications
- AI security, compliance, and governance aligned with UK Government and NCSC standards
- Prevention of AI hallucinations through guardrails and validation
- Enterprise implementation strategies

## Programme Structure

### Five-Week Intensive Curriculum

**Week 1: Foundations of AI and Prompt Engineering** (37.5 hours)
- Understanding AI, machine learning, and large language models
- Basic to advanced prompt engineering techniques
- AI hallucination prevention and guardrails
- RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) fundamentals

**Week 2: Claude Code Mastery** (37.5 hours)
- Installation, configuration, and architecture
- Common coding workflows and git integration
- Creating custom skills and artifacts
- Multi-agent system design and orchestration
- CLAUDE.md project optimization

**Week 3: Microsoft 365 Copilot** (37.5 hours)
- Copilot across Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams
- Advanced prompting techniques for M365
- Team collaboration and organizational adoption
- Security and privacy considerations

**Week 4: AI Security, Compliance, and Governance** (37.5 hours)
- UK Government Secure by Design for AI
- NCSC Secure Design Principles (all four lifecycle phases)
- AI governance frameworks and risk management
- Data privacy, ethical AI, and continuous monitoring

**Week 5: Integration and Real-World Application** (37.5 hours)
- Enterprise AI implementation strategy
- Hands-on capstone project integrating all concepts
- Final comprehensive assessment
- Personal AI best practices handbook creation

## Compliance and Standards Alignment

### UK Government Requirements

**Secure by Design Principles:**
- Security built into AI systems from inception
- Continuous security throughout lifecycle
- Accountability and transparency

**AI Playbook (February 2025):**
- 10 principles for safe and effective AI use
- Public sector guidance applicable to enterprises
- Risk-based approach to AI deployment

**AI Cyber Security Standard (January 2025):**
- 13 principles for protecting AI systems
- World-first voluntary code of practice
- Critical infrastructure protection

### NCSC Secure Design Principles

**Four Lifecycle Phases:**
1. **Secure Design** - Risk assessment, threat modeling, architecture decisions
2. **Secure Development** - Secure coding, testing, validation
3. **Secure Deployment** - Configuration management, security hardening
4. **Secure Operations** - Continuous monitoring, incident response

**International Collaboration:**
- Joint guidelines with CISA (US)
- 21+ international agencies and ministries
- Global standard for secure AI development
